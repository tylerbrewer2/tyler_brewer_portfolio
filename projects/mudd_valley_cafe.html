Branding Design- Mudd Valley

Software: Adobe Illustrator

Typeface: Proxima Nova, Wisdom Script

Rebranding for the Mudd Valley Cafe located in Holmes County, the center of Amish community life in Ohio. The logo visually mimics the atmosphere of the shop with the use of vintage colors and bold text. Won an American Graphic Design Award from Graphic Design USA and was featured in Graphic Design USA December 2015 issue, seen by over 100,000 working design professionals.
