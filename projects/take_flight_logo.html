Branding Design- Take Flight

Software: Adobe Illustrator

Typeface: Brandon Grotesque

The client sells all-natural products so I wanted the logo to spring from something natural as well. The mark represents wind and gives movement to the overall logo. The tag-line "Discover Your Comfort" eludes to the notion that consumers can find simple comfort and luxury just by using Take Flight products.
