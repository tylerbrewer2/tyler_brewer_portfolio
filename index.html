I'm a web designer and developer. I exist to make your life easier.

WANNA KNOW HOW I GOT THESE SCARS?

I don’t actually have any nasty scars, but I can make you a cool website and I do love Batman.

I graduated from Mount Vernon Nazarene University with a BA in Graphic Design in 2014. After working as a graphic designer in Amish Country for a year, I put my love for design and my love for development together and chose to attend The Flatiron School in New York City.

I’m obsessed with simplistically sophisticated design and love seeing how design and development can go together to make something truly fascinating and difference making.
